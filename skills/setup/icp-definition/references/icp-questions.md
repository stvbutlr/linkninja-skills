# ICP Question Bank

Complete question set organized by dimension. Use 1-2 questions per turn during the interview — don't dump all at once.

## Role & Industry

| Question | What It Reveals |
|----------|----------------|
| "What job titles do your best clients typically have?" | Headline keywords for `scan_connections` |
| "What industry or sector are they in?" | Industry filter terms |
| "What company size — employees or revenue range?" | Scale indicator |
| "What does their LinkedIn headline usually look like?" | Direct scan keyword source |
| "Are they the decision maker or do they influence the decision?" | Authority vs champion distinction |

## Life Stage & Transitions

| Question | What It Reveals |
|----------|----------------|
| "What moment or transition are they going through right now?" | Trigger event — why now? |
| "Are they scaling, struggling, just got promoted, just lost a key hire?" | Urgency level |
| "What is the trigger that makes them start looking for help?" | Opening message angle |
| "What changed in their business in the last 3-6 months?" | Recency of need |

## Daily Frustrations

| Question | What It Reveals |
|----------|----------------|
| "What problem does your ideal client deal with every day or every week?" | High-frequency pain = high responsiveness |
| "What's the moment in their day where they feel the pain most?" | Emotional hook for messages |
| "What do they complain about in DMs or on calls — not publicly?" | Private language vs public language |
| "What would they Google at 11pm when they can't sleep?" | Core anxiety |
| "What problem do they think about in the shower?" | Top-of-mind concern |

### Pain Frequency Filter

| Frequency | Responsiveness | Example | Priority |
|-----------|---------------|---------|----------|
| Daily | Very high | "Opens Slack at 7am and nothing moves" | Target first |
| Weekly | High | "Sunday night dread about Monday review" | Strong target |
| Monthly | Moderate | "Monthly board report shows churn climbing" | Viable |
| Annual | Low | "Annual planning feels overwhelming" | Deprioritize |

## Aspirations

| Question | What It Reveals |
|----------|----------------|
| "What are they trying to become or achieve in 6-12 months?" | Value proposition framing |
| "What would their life look like if this problem was solved?" | Desired outcome |
| "What kind of professional do they want to be seen as?" | Identity aspiration |

## Language & Jargon

| Question | What It Reveals |
|----------|----------------|
| "What acronyms or technical terms do insiders use?" | Insider vocabulary for DMs |
| "What tools or platforms do they use daily?" | Tech stack signals |
| "How do they describe this problem in their own words?" | Natural language for messages |
| "What books, podcasts, or thought leaders do they follow?" | Cultural reference points |

## Cultural Context

| Question | What It Reveals |
|----------|----------------|
| "Where are most of your clients located?" | Geographic/timezone targeting |
| "Are they formal or casual in how they communicate?" | Tone baseline |
| "What would feel 'off' or pushy in their world?" | Trust violations to avoid |
| "What does credibility look like — credentials, case studies, peer referrals?" | Proof type to lead with |

## ICP-to-Keyword Translation

After the interview, extract headline keywords for `scan_connections`:

| ICP Element | Headline Signal | Keyword Examples |
|-------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Service offering | What they sell or help with | "Coach", "Consultant", "Advisor", "Strategist" |
| Niche / vertical | Specific area | "Recruitment", "Wealth", "Leadership", "Career", "SEO" |
| Business model | How they operate | "Founder", "Owner", "Solo", "Boutique", "Independent" |
| Industry / sector (if needed) | Broader context | "Financial Services", "Professional Services", "Healthcare", "Creative" |
| Seniority (when targeting buyers in firms) | Level markers | "Director", "VP", "Partner", "Principal" |

Common exclude keywords:
- "Student", "Intern", "Looking for opportunities", "Seeking", "Open to work"
- "Retired", "Board Member" (if targeting active operators)
- Competitor titles or irrelevant industries
